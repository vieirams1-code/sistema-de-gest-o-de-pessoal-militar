# Palette's Journal

## 2025-05-15 - Accessibilty on Icon-only buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons in the Efetivo table were missing ARIA labels and visual tooltips, making them inaccessible for screen readers and potentially confusing for new users.
**Action:** Always wrap icon-only buttons with Tooltip components and add descriptive aria-labels to ensure both visual and screen-reader accessibility.
